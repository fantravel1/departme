Questions

Frequently asked

Everything you want to know about mood-based travel.

When you're burned out, DepartMe recommends a "Disappear" journey — quiet coastal towns like the Azores, forest retreats in rural Japan, or the Scottish Highlands. These itineraries pair low-stimulation environments with rituals like morning silence, analog cafes, ocean walks, and ambient soundscapes to help your nervous system decompress.

Heartbreak calls for a "Heal" journey. DepartMe builds itineraries around places that hold space for grief without forcing positivity — think Kyoto's temple gardens, Portugal's quiet Alentejo coast, or the stillness of rural Iceland. Each journey includes daily rituals like tea ceremonies, journaling prompts, and sunset walks designed to move through sadness, not around it.

DepartMe's "Disappear" journeys are built exactly for this. They take you to places where you can drop off the grid — volcanic islands, remote coastal villages, deep forest retreats. Itineraries include 3-day and 7-day options with minimal planning required, paired with rituals for solitude and sensory quiet.

Feeling stuck calls for a "Motion" journey. DepartMe pairs cities with strong walking culture — Lisbon's hills, Tokyo's endless neighborhoods, Mexico City's layered districts — with nature that physically moves you: coastal trails, mountain passes, river walks. The goal is to break the feeling of stagnation through literal and emotional movement.

Anxiety and overwhelm respond to DepartMe's "Quiet" journeys — destinations chosen for low sensory load, gentle natural environments, and slow daily rhythms. Think Norwegian fjord towns, rural Provence, or New Zealand's South Island. Each itinerary includes breathwork spots, low-crowd timing, and ambient sound pairings to calm the nervous system.

A fresh start is a "Leap" journey. DepartMe builds these around cities that reward reinvention — Berlin, Buenos Aires, Taipei, Marrakech. Itineraries include neighborhood exploration, conversations with locals, creative workshops, and morning rituals designed to help you step into a new version of yourself.

Loneliness calls for a "Belong" journey. DepartMe designs itineraries around places with strong communal culture — Italian piazzas, Japanese onsen towns, Colombian village markets, Portuguese tascas. These journeys prioritize third places (cafes, markets, public squares) and gentle social rituals that create connection without pressure.

Numbness responds to DepartMe's "Wake Up" journeys — designed around sensory-rich, high-contrast environments that jolt your emotional system back online. Think Fez's medina, Naples' chaos, Oaxaca's colors, or Iceland's geothermal landscapes. Itineraries include spice markets, cold water immersion, sunrise hikes, and live music — experiences that bypass the head and hit the body.

DepartMe is built for exactly this — emotional reset through travel, not therapy. Every journey combines place, ritual, and rhythm to help you shift your mental state. The best reset trips pair a low-stimulation natural environment (coast, forest, mountains) with a slow-paced city, daily rituals (walking, tea, journaling, silence), and ambient soundscapes. It's experience design, not treatment.

On DepartMe, you start by choosing how you feel — burned out, heartbroken, restless, curious, lonely, stuck, numb, or celebrating. Based on your mood, we match you with a journey theme (Disappear, Heal, Escape, Explore, Leap, Belong, Quiet, Motion, Wake Up, or Radiate) and build a full itinerary with city and nature pairings, daily rhythms, cafes, walking routes, rituals, and soundscapes.
